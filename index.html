Colombia

Guatapé is a colorful town in Colombia, known for its decorated buildings. It sits on the shores of the Embalse Guatapé, a reservoir. A major attraction is the Piedra del Peñol, a towering rock with a staircase for panoramic views. It's a popular destination for its picturesque scenery and cultural charm.
